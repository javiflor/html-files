Commited to Making Things Right

The Paws N Tails Center was established in 2015 to serve the South Bay area. The PNTC offers pet adoptions, medical care services, pet training, and first-time pet owner education. At PNTC, we care about finding the right fit for the right owner.

Our mission is rescue lost and abandoned animals in our local community, assist animals by providing food, shelter and vet care to those in need, promoting spay and neutering to end the population of unwanted pets, and provide adoption opportunities of animals to loving homes.

Our vision to strive for a world where every animal has the medical help it needs to sustain longevity and a forever loving-home.

Become a foster volunteer

Why Foster?

Fostering allows you to temporarily open your home to an animal which allows our shelter to take in more animals in need of help.

In addition, fostering offers the one-on-one attention and loving comfort that only a home setting can offer, which is precisely what some animals need to heal.

As a foster volunteer , you may host animals with special medical or behavioral needs, mother animals with nursing litters or underage animals who need special feeding and socialization, like puppies, bunnies or kittens. By fostering an animal you are providing rehabilitation in a nurturing home environment, directly preparing them for adoption into a loving home.

Categories of Fosterable Animals

    - Bottle kittens (frequent need)
    - Kittens under 4 weeks of age, feeding/stimulating every 3 hours, even over night
    - Quarantine animals (frequent need) - Panleuk releases etc.
    - Medical assignments (occasional need) - recovering from injury or surgery
    - Behavior Assignments (Occasional need) - on behavior modification plans
    - Bottle puppies (occasional need) - Puppies under 4 weeks of age, feeding/stimulating every
    3 hours, even over night
    - Older puppies (occasional need) -Must have an closed yard since pups cannot go out on
    walks due to immature immune systems. If no yard then must stay indoors and be potty trained
    - Large breed pregnant dogs (occasional need) - must have a quite, dedicated room away
    from other pets and children
    - Small pets (occasional need) - hamsters, bunnies, guinea pigs

Commited to Making Things Right

The Paws N Tails Center was established in 2015 to serve the South Bay area. The PNTC offers pet adoptions, medical care services, pet training, and first-time pet owner education. At PNTC, we care about finding the right fit for the right owner.

Our mission is rescue lost and abandoned animals in our local community, assist animals by providing food, shelter and vet care to those in need, promoting spay and neutering to end the population of unwanted pets, and provide adoption opportunities of animals to loving homes.

Our vision to strive for a world where every animal has the medical help it needs to sustain longevity and a forever loving-home.
